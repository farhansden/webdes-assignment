About Gulmarg

Gulmarg is a stunning hill station and popular skiing destination located in the Pir Panjal range of the western Himalayas, in the Indian union territory of Jammu and Kashmir.

Key Information

Popularity High (especially during winter for skiing)
Distance from Srinagar About 50 km (31 miles)
Best time to visit December to March (for skiing), April to June (for summer activities)

Transportation Options

Mode Approx. Cost (INR)
Taxi ₹1500 - ₹2000
Shared Taxi ₹500 - ₹700
Bus ₹100 - ₹150

Nearby Attractions

  • Gulmarg Gondola (World's second-highest operating cable car)
  • Alpather Lake (13 km trek from Gulmarg)
  • Khilanmarg (6 km from Gulmarg, offers panoramic views)

Gulmarg Ski Trip Packing List


    Essential items for your Gulmarg ski trip:
    
    - Warm, waterproof ski jacket and pants
    - Thermal base layers and fleece mid-layers
    - Ski goggles and sunglasses
    - Warm, waterproof gloves or mittens
    - Thick, warm socks
    - Ski boots (can be rented in Gulmarg)
    - Skis or snowboard (can be rented in Gulmarg)
    - Helmet (strongly recommended, can be rented)
    - Sunscreen and lip balm with high SPF
    - Backpack for carrying essentials on the slopes
    - Camera or GoPro for capturing memories
